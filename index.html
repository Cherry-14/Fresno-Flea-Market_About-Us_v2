Flea Market Story
When you visit our Fresno flea market, you'll spend the day having fun, meeting local vendors, and finding amazing deals on new and used items. Many times, a family will come to our swap meet empty-handed and find amazing deals on unique and local items that they would have never found on a shopping trip anywhere else.
